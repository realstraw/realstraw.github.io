-- Ryan Holiday, _The Obstacle Is The Way_

_As horrific as the disease was, public officials and the media helped create
the terror ... by minimizing it, by trying to reassure… If there’s a single
dominant lesson from 1918, it’s that ... You don’t manage the truth. You tell

movies build upon the fear of the unknown ... once the the monster appears,

dissipates._

-- John Barry, _The Great Influenza_
